Professional Summary

I am a graphic designer with three years of experience who is passionate about designing digital and print things for the world to see. Resourceful and multidisciplinary with a penchant for the creative arts including film, motion graphics, photography and web design.  As an organised and highly creative individual, I love to come up with challenging marketing ideas that grab people’s attention to show appealing visuals most uniquely and attractively.

Skills

Competent in designing original creative, as well as working with and modifying creative initiated by others. Possesses an extensive design toolbox with competencies in software such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, After Effects, etc. Also a creative thinker, detail-oriented, confident, and able to express ideas and concepts in an articulate, well-thought-out manner. Most importantly passionate about designing and creating a final product that you can be proud of.
